FAQ

  • Can we walk in without registering?
  • Registration is recommended but not required. You may register at 10:00am on 21st at Harper Library Building Entrance.
  • If I can't attend the entire conference, should I still come?
  • Absolutely! We recommend students to attend the whole conference because as you can see from our full agenda, we focus on executing and creating teams to support your venture. However, if you couldn't make it, we highly recommend you to attend:
    21st May 2011
    • 11:30am Keynote Speaker- Dr. Peter Slowe at Harper 130
    • 1:30pm Guest Speaker- Mr. Donald Laackman at Cobb 307
    22nd May 2011
    • 11:00am EnvisionDo Bazaar at Hutch (Free Brunch provided)

Agenda

21st May 2011

  • 10:00am Registration at Harper Library Building Entrance
  • 10:30am Kick-off and speed networking (teams begin to self-form) Harper 130
  • 11:30am Keynote speaker- Dr. Peter Slowe Harper 130
  • 12:30pm Lunch and Startup Generation (Pitch preparation) McCormick
  • 1:30pm Guest Speaker- Mr. Donald Laackman McCormick
  • 2:30pm Project Development (Quick pitches and feedback, domain registration, focused team tasks) MrCormick
  • 4:30pm Dance workshop Cobb-various rooms
  • 5:50pm International Fiesta (teams perform their dance and continue to develop their project/venture during dinner) Harper130

22nd May 2011

  • 10:50am Registration at Hutchinson Commons
  • 11:00am EnvisionDo Bazaar with Brunch Hutch
  • 12:30pm Closing: Initiative Launch and moving forward Hutch
  • Conference officially ends at 2:00pm, but teams are welcome to stay after to continue development their project until 4:00pm
